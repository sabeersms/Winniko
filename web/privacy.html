Privacy Policy for Winniko

Last Updated: January 2026

At Winniko, accessible from our mobile application, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Winniko and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our service:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Profile Assets: Profile pictures (collected via your Camera or Gallery permissions).
  • Location Data: Approximate location data to localize competition listings and organizer details.
  • Usage Activity: Your predictions, competition participation, and app interactions.
  • Technical Data: Device identifiers, IP address, and crash reports for performance monitoring.

2. Payment Information

If you participate in paid contests, we use third-party payment processors (specifically Razorpay). We do not store your credit card or bank details on our servers. All financial transactions are processed securely by Razorpay in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.

3. Third-Party Services & Ads

We use third-party SDKs that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): Used to serve advertisements. They may collect device identifiers and usage data to personalize your ad experience.
  • Firebase: Used for authentication, database, storage, and push notifications.

4. How We Use Information

We use your data to:

  • Manage your account and competition rankings.
  • Process entries and score calculations.
  • Notify you about match results and competition updates.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure a fair playing field.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You have the right to delete your account and all associated personal data at any time. Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account inside the app. This action is irreversible and will permanently remove your profile, competition history, and indices from our database.

6. Children's Privacy

Winniko does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately for deletion.

7. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@winniko.com.
